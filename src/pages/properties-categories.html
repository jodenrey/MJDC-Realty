List of subdivisions

Idesia Units

The Idesia SJDM is a 15.84-hectare project and will offer 1,281 housing units meticulously designed for young families and professionals who aspire to become homeowners near Metro Manila. Interested buyers and potential homeowners may expect the same community concept introduced by the joint venture in other Idesia City projects in Southern Luzon. It will feature the signature designs: Yama (townhouse) and Sora (single-attached).

Amaresa Marilao Units

Along one of Marilao’s busy main avenues is Amaresa, a 6.5 hectare first class residential community that sits on a prime and convenient location, as it is only a kilometer away to shopping centers Puregold and Savemore. Get easy access to public transportation as buses ply along the main avenue and the Muzon Central Terminal also just a kilometer away. Truly, everything you need are all within the comforts of your dream home.

Sentrina Alaminos Units

Sentrina Alaminos brings Class, Comfort, and Convenience to the Alaminos living space. Perfectly situated along Maharlika National Highway in the progressive municipality of Alaminos, Laguna, just 2 kilometers from SM City San Pablo. This 18-hectare master planned development elevates your lifestyle with its first class residential community, elegant Modern Contemporary homes, resort-inspired amenities, and a super-prime expansive commercial area to redefine convenient living.

Alegria Units

A residential ebtalishment situated in Marilao,Bulacan. This is the most exclusive subdicision of the region that offers a comfortable living to its people. Lush green sorroundings, beautifully designed townhouse, Duplex, Single Attached and Single Dettached, Luxurious amenities, Convenient Features, and various nearby landmarks are what one gets to avail and enjoy while investing in this excellent residential community.

Tierra San Jose Units

Tierra San Jose offers an exceptional living experience in a prime location. This residential community features thoughtfully designed homes with modern amenities and sustainable features. With its strategic location, residents enjoy easy access to major transportation hubs, commercial centers, and educational institutions, making it an ideal choice for families and professionals seeking a balanced lifestyle.

Alexandra Heights Units

Alexandra Heights represents luxury living at its finest. This premium residential development offers spacious units with high-end finishes and spectacular views. The community features resort-style amenities including swimming pools, fitness centers, and landscaped gardens. Located in a developing urban center, Alexandra Heights provides the perfect balance of privacy and convenience for discerning homeowners.

Bluhomes Katmon Units

Bluhomes Katmon represents the future of sustainable living. This innovative residential project features eco-friendly homes designed with energy-efficient systems and green building materials. The community offers a range of unit types to accommodate different lifestyles, all within a carefully planned neighborhood with communal spaces and lush landscapes, promoting both environmental responsibility and community connection.

Regent Heights Units

Regent Heights is a 4.5 hectare residential community in San Jose Del Monte City by Pinnacle Homes. This gated subdivision features affordable townhouse units with three bedrooms, lifestyle amenities, and convenience shops for daily essentials. Perfect for professionals and starting families looking for an idyllic residence that’s affordable, spacious, and close to Metro Manila.
